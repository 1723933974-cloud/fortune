What is a Yes/No Oracle?

A Yes/No Oracle is a simple divination tool that helps you get clarity on decisions. When you're feeling stuck or torn between options, sometimes all you need is a clear signal from the universe.

This oracle randomly generates a Yes or No answer with equal probability. While the result is random, many people find that their reaction to the answer — whether they feel relief or disappointment — reveals what they truly wanted all along.

Use it for fun decisions: should I try that new restaurant? Should I call that person? Should I take the day off? Let the oracle give you a nudge!

How to Use

1. Think of a clear yes/no question in your mind.

2. Take a breath and focus on the question.

3. Tap "Ask the Oracle" to receive your answer.

4. Notice your first emotional reaction — that's often your true answer.
